Asahi

Asahi Super Dry is the world’s first super dry beer. Curiosity led us to invent a crisp, clean tasting beer that truly embodies the refreshing taste of Modern Japan. As Japan’s interest in rugby has grown, so has our investment in the sport. The successful partnership with Rugby World Cup will continue through a long-term commitment as Official Beer and Principal Partner of all tournaments until 2029. This includes Women’s Rugby World Cup England 2025 and Australia 2029, as well as Men’s Rugby World Cup Australia 2027. The three-tournament partnership with World Rugby will see Asahi Super Dry foster a cross-culture activation platform showcasing rugby like never before, bringing intrigue and a fresh perspective to rugby fans around the globe.

Emirates

We’re proud to be back at the Rugby World Cup for another exciting tournament, flying fans across the world in comfort to watch the matches.

As one of the world’s largest international airlines, we fly travellers to over 140 destinations through Dubai. Just like the rugby, those special moments on board turn into lifelong memories.

As a proud supporter of the game since 1987, our Rugby World Cup journey began with our role as a Tournament Sponsor of Rugby World Cup 2007 in France. We strengthened this commitment in 2011 by becoming a Worldwide Partner, an elite status we have proudly maintained for every Rugby World Cup since.

Through our sponsorship of the World Rugby referees, we’re at the centre of every international rugby match. The elite pool of officials who take charge of the sport’s biggest matches wear Emirates on their shirts, ensuring we are with them at the heart of the action on the pitch and on the touchlines too.

Our passion for the game spans continents and competitions. We’re the Title Sponsor of the Dubai Sevens and the Cape Town Sevens. And we support European Professional Club Rugby, Asia Rugby and the UAE Rugby Federation, bringing people together through a love of sport.

Whether you’re flying to the tournament or on your next holiday, your journey there is just as unforgettable. Look forward to spacious seats, private suites and lie-flat comfort. Enjoy delicious regional meals and complimentary drinks. And recline to some of the best entertainment in the skies – including live sport. There’s even a shower spa and a bar on the Emirates A380. Discover more on emirates.com.

Liqui Moly

With around 4,000 items, LIQUI MOLY offers a global, uniquely broad range of automotive chemicals: motor oils and additives, greases and pastes, sprays and car care, glues and sealants. Since 1957 LIQUI MOLY has been developing and producing its premium products for an international market that now comprises more than 150 countries.

As a globally operating company, the expansion of LIQUI MOLY’s international brand awareness is the focus of any sponsorship activity. That’s why the lubricant specialist is thrilled to partner with the World Rugby Cup for the very first time and reach new enthusiastic sporting audiences worldwide.

Find out more: https://www.liqui-moly.com/en/

Dove Men

Dove Men+Care™ offers a range of personal care products, specially formulated for men to deliver high performance and superior skincare.

We’re proud to be the Official Body Care Supplier of Rugby World Cup 2027, extending our strong heritage in rugby union to the sport’s pinnacle tournament – Rugby World Cup.

Dove Men+Care™ and other Unilever Personal Care brands, such as Sure, Dove and Paula’s Choice, launched the three-tournament partnership at the record-breaking Women’s Rugby World Cup England 2025 and we look forward to supporting World Rugby break new records at the upcoming men’s and women’s tournaments in 2027 and 2029 respectively.

Learn more about Dove Men+Care™ on our website: https://www.dove.com/uk/men-care.html

Gilbert

Gilbert Rugby has been the official match ball of every Rugby World Cup since 1995 and are delighted to supply the balls for Rugby World Cup Australia in 2027 and 2029. Gilbert have crafted an exceptional ball to celebrate the 2027 tournament that encapsulate the look and feel inspired by Australian and first nations culture. The official match ball will be launched in 2026 for international match use – to familiarize players with the ball – ahead of RWC 2027.

Lipovitan D

Taisho Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd. is Japan’s largest over-the-counter (OTC) pharmaceutical company, with a proud legacy of more than a century in this field. Today, we operate in over 80 countries worldwide, focusing primarily on OTC medicines across Southeast Asia and Europe.

Among our many flagship brands, Lipovitan D stands out as a pioneer—widely regarded as the original energy drink.

Since 2001, we have supported the Japan National Rugby Team, and since fiscal year 2022, we have served as a Premium Global Partner of the New Zealand Rugby Union, contributing to the growth and promotion of rugby worldwide. In addition, we proudly sponsored the Rugby World Cup as an Official Sponsor for the 2019 and as an Official Supplier for the 2023 tournaments, and we will continue this commitment for the 2027 tournament, helping energize this global sporting event.

Meiji

Meiji Co., Ltd. is one of Japan’s leading food manufacturers, handling a wide range of products loved by all generations.

Based on the principles of “safety and security”, “deliciousness and fun”, and “health and nutrition”, we contribute to healthy eating habits by delivering a diverse array of products – including formula milk, milk and dairy products, confectionery, sports nutrition foods, and liquid foods – to people of all ages, from infants to the elderly.

Mitsubishi Estate

Mitsubishi Estate Co., Ltd. is a comprehensive real estate company, established in 1937. With “contribution to society through urban development” as its basic mission, the company engages in the development, leasing, and management of office buildings and commercial properties, typified by the cluster of buildings in Tokyo’s Marunouchi district, the development of real estate for investment purposes, the development and sale of residential properties, design supervision, real estate brokerage services, international businesses, and investment management business.

Building on this extensive experience, Mitsubishi Estate entered the Australian market in 2016 with the participation of “Melbourne Quarter East Tower,” a large-scale residential development, and subsequently established its Australia branch in 2021. Drawing on its global expertise, the company is committed to enhancing its presence in Australia by building a high-quality, diversified investment portfolio.

Fanatics

Fanatics is a leading global digital sports platform. The company ignites the passions of global sports fans and maximises the presence and reach for hundreds of sports partners globally by offering innovative products and services across Fanatics Commerce, Fanatics Collectibles, and Fanatics Betting & Gaming, allowing sports fans to Buy, Collect and Bet.

Through the Fanatics platform, sports fans can buy licensed fan gear, jerseys, lifestyle and streetwear products, headwear, and hardgoods; collect physical and digital trading cards, sports memorabilia, and other digital assets; and bet as the company builds its Sportsbook and iGaming platform. Fanatics has an established database of over 100 million global sports fans, a global partner network with over 900 sports properties, including major national and international professional sports leagues, teams, players associations, athletes, celebrities, colleges, and college conferences.

For additional information please visit www.fanaticsinc.com.

Ticketmaster

Ticketmaster Sport is the global leader in sports ticketing, providing advanced digital ticketing technology and services for major live events worldwide. It partners with clubs, leagues, and federations to deliver seamless fan experience, fan-first ticketing and secure entry solutions. Most notably, Ticketmaster Sport has been named the official ticketing partner of the Rugby World Cup 2027, selected by World Rugby to manage ticketing for the Men’s Rugby World Cup in Australia. This partnership aims to enhance the fan experience, reflecting its pivotal role in one of rugby’s biggest global events.

Childfund

Rugby brings people together – across clubs, communities, and cultures. Built on respect and solidarity, it creates moments that last long after the final whistle. As a Social Impact Supporter of Men’s Rugby World Cup 2027, ChildFund Rugby works with partners worldwide to build safer, more inclusive communities – all powered by rugby. Our partnerships have already reached more than 85,000 children, with a goal of 500,000 by 2030 – proving the game’s impact goes far beyond match day. Rugby unites us. RWC 2027 takes it further.

Learn more at www.childfundrugby.org.
