Ticket pricing structure

Each Match Ticket consists of two financial components: 

  1. The Ticket Face Value (official ticket price including GST). 
  2. The Distribution Rights Fee (DRF), payable for the right to package and distribute the ticket within an Official Travel Package.

Distribution Rights Fees vary by: 

  • Match (e.g. pool match vs semi-final vs final) 
  • Ticket category 
  • Match band classification

Sales & order procedure

All tickets must be sold strictly as part of an Official Travel Package. “Ticket-only” sales are not permitted. 

Travel Package means the combination of at least one Ticket with the provision by (or on behalf of) the OTA of either a Return Travel Mechanism or Accommodation (or both) for the purpose of the Travel Client attending and viewing the relevant match (or matches) at RWC27. 

Return Travel Mechanism: means return travel by air, sea, train, or private transfer. The minimum distance for return travel is 100km to and 100km from the Venue.
Orders must:

  • Be submitted via the Agent Portal (or Account Manager via order form prior to launch)
  • Be formally accepted by RWCE
  • Be fully paid prior to ticket release 
  • No order is valid until confirmed by RWCE in writing.

Ticket allocation mechanics

Ticket allocations are subject to availability and confirmation by RWCE. We base this on a first in, first serve basis and we will do our best to accommodate any special requests however they cannot be guaranteed until time of allocation closer to the tournament. 

Please note:

  • Your full allocation for a match may not be seated together.
  • RWCE will use reasonable endeavours to accommodate grouping requests but cannot guarantee contiguous seating 
  • Additional ticket requests beyond your Minimum Commitment are subject to availability and formal approval.

Distribution Rights Fee

The Distribution Rights Fee is payable in addition to the Ticket Face Value 

The DRF:

  • Grants you the right to package the ticket within an Official Travel Package.
  • Varies by match band and ticket category.
  • Is contractually binding once an order is confirmed.

If a match is cancelled before it takes place, the DRF may be refunded. In other circumstances, the DRF remains payable.

In Summary 

Cancelled before match day: DRF refunded.

Rescheduled, relocated, or otherwise amended: DRF remains payable.

Volume discount policy

Volume discounts apply to Distribution Rights Fees only and are outlined within Schedule 4 of the Agreement. 

Discount levels are determined by:

  • Total ticket commitment value
  • Match band distribution
  • Multi-match participation

The OTA will receive a Volume Discount on Match Ticket after the Multi-Match Discount on the Distribution Rights Fees is applied based on the number of Tickets purchase in initial ticket order, as follows; 

Number of Tickets across selected rounds  Percentage of discount on Discounted Distribution Rights Fee 
0-2,999 0%
3,000-8,999 5%
9,000-14,999 10%
15,000-20,999 15%
21,000+ 20%
  • Volume discounts will only apply once the relevant volume threshold has been reached and will not apply retrospectively to Tickets purchased under that threshold. For example, if an OTA purchases 3,500 Tickets the OTA will only receive the 5% volume discount on those Tickets exceeding 3,000. For the avoidance of doubt, volume discounts will only be applicable to Distribution Rights Fees and not to the Ticket Face Value or to the Category 1 ticket premium, as defined below.
  • Volume discounts do not apply to purchases of Official Hospitality Packages or Category 1 Match Ticket Orders

Wheelchair Access

Wheelchair Ticketing Process – Key Information

Note: Wheelchair accessible tickets are sold as a package consisting of the wheelchair user plus one companion.

See pricing

  • Pricing:
    Wheelchair tickets are priced at the lowest reserved seat category for the relevant match.
  • Companion Ticket:
    Each wheelchair ticket includes one complimentary companion ticket.
    • Companion seating will be adjacent or nearby, subject to availability.
    • Due to venue configurations, exact positioning may vary.
  • Seating Setup:
    • Wheelchair bays are designated spaces (no physical seat)and require the guest to bring their own wheelchair.
    • Companion tickets include a standard seat.
  • Companion Requirements:
    Companion ticket holders must be 18 years or older.
  • Additional Tickets:
    Any additional tickets within the same group will be:
    • Charged at full price, and

Seated as close as possible to the wheelchair allocation (subject to venue layout).

Request & Approval Process

  • All wheelchair requests must be submitted via to your Account Manager and this will be processed through to World Rugby’s Wheelchair Bay Request Form.
  • This process allows us to review, approve, and track all requests, including required quantities for wheelchair and companion tickets.

Hospitality Considerations

  • For wheelchair/accessible patrons, the ticket component is charged at the lowest ticket category rate, while the hospitality component remains at full price.
  • For carers/companions, only the ticket face value is waived; the hospitality component remains payable in full.
  • This pricing structure applies for internal reporting and settlements, including hospitality products.
  • OTAs are requested to contact their dedicated Account Manager to confirm accessibility pricing and availability before presenting options to clients.

Ticket Swaps

Within the new order process, tickets can be exchanged up until October 2026. After this point, once the portal is live, an administration fee will apply to any ticket exchanges (further details to be confirmed), provided the replacement tickets are of equal or higher Distribution Rights Fee value.

For ticket swaps, please contact your Account Manager to request the Ticket Swap Form.

Payment schedule

The OTA is responsible for:

  • Ticket Face Value
  • Distribution Rights Fees
  • Curated Experience pricing (where applicable)

All payments must be made in AUD via bank transfer. All sums due from the OTA shall be paid exclusive of any applicable bank charges, which charges shall be borne by the OTA. RWCE shall be entitled to charge interest on overdue payments at the rate of two per cent (2% per annum) above Reserve Bank of Australia base cash rate from time to time from the due date until the date of payment
